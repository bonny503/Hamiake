Privacy & safety

How Hamiake protects tenants and landlords.

Hamiake Rentals Kenya is built around verified listings, clear landlord contacts, admin approval, and simple reporting tools to reduce fake house listings and suspicious agents.

Tenant Safety

  • Confirm the house physically before paying rent or deposit.
  • Use the listed landlord WhatsApp number to ask for fresh photos, location hints, and viewing time.
  • Do not send ID documents or private financial details to unverified agents.
  • Report listings with fake photos, wrong rent, wrong location, or suspicious payment requests.

Landlord Verification

  • Landlord posts go through admin approval before appearing publicly.
  • Admin may request phone, email, payment confirmation, and listing proof before activation.
  • Verified landlord badges are reserved for accounts that pass review.
  • Listings can be removed when tenants report fraud or false information.

Payments

  • Landlord subscription payments use PayBill 247247 and account 740532.
  • For USDT, USDC, RLUSD, or BTC payments, request wallet details from admin before sending crypto.
  • Send payment confirmation to admin on WhatsApp for manual account approval.
  • Tenants should not pay deposits before confirming the house and landlord details.

Data We Collect

  • Landlord name, email, phone number, subscription plan, and listing details entered on the website.
  • Tenant report details submitted through the report listing form.
  • Contact actions may open WhatsApp or phone apps controlled by the user's device.
  • The current static MVP does not store secure passwords on a backend yet.

Need help or want to report a listing?

Contact Hamiake admin directly. Include the listing name, landlord name, location, and the issue you noticed.
